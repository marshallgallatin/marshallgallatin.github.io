## Tip of the Week ##

You should double check those MD5 hashes given with downloaded software.  Recently, Linux Mint's webservers were [hacked](http://blog.linuxmint.com/?p=2994), and a hacked Linux Mint ISO was uploaded that contained a backdoor.  I use Linux Mint on my laptop, so I found this news particularly interesting.  Granted, had the hackers updated the MD5 hashes as well, it would have been very difficult to figure it out.
